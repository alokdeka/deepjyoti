About Deepjyoti -

Deep Jyoti Saikia

PROFESSIONAL

An Assamese guy from Guwahati (Assam), who is promoting about health and fitness since the year 2013. The goal of my blogs is to educate people and motivate them for adopting a healthy lifestyle. I started my childhood days playing the sport that is the most famous in India, Cricket. I started playing cricket at a very young age and represented my school and college in the respective teams. As a kid I was very much into fitness and sports. During my junior college degree, I started going to the gym and loved the gymming atmosphere. In 2017, I went to associate with Gold’s Gym, Shillong, working till present!

During my career, I dealt with a number of clients and have fulfilled their goals respectively. I trained clients who came for Weight Loss, General Fitness, CrossFit, Fat Loss, Strength and Conditioning and helped people having chronic health issues with special needs like Fibromyalgia ,Cancer, workout during pregnancy, Rheumatoid Arthritis,Hypothyroidism, PCOS, Slipped discs, Sports injuries etc.
